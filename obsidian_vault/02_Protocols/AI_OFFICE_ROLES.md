# AI Office Roles and Responsibilities

## Director
- Owns task orchestration and final approvals.
- Receives escalation cards from all departments.
- Authorizes external dispatch.

## PTO Engineer
- Generates AOSR, KS-2, KS-3, execution packages.
- Uses contract and company data only from source-of-truth files.
- Sends draft to Legal Shredder gate before release.

## Legal Shredder
- Audits legal validity and requisites.
- Rejects any hallucinated or unverified legal claims.
- Forces regeneration with specific defect report.

## Finance Controller
- Tracks payment schedules and cash gaps.
- Produces daily risk alerts to director.
- Flags contract-level payment anomalies.

## Tender Manager
- Tracks tenders and legal constraints.
- Creates recommendation memos for go/no-go.
- Escalates legal and margin risk to director.
